FAQ

How to use this platform?
To use this platform on PC, TronLink extension must be installed on your Google Chrome browser. If you are using smartphone TokenPocket [iOS] or TokenPocket [Android] is suitable. Put some TRX in your wallet and begin!
Tip:
When interacting with smart contracts, it's always better to freeze some TRX to gain Energy. This way energy will be consumed when triggering smart contract's functions, instead of TRX.
How does the game work?
The staking contract drips 2% of the staking pool daily to TEWKEN stakers
250k to 1 million TEWKEN is allocated to the staking pool every 2 months
The staking contract also rewards TRX as a bonus to stakers
INFINITEW and Crazy will provide external source of revenue to the contract
What are the fees?
0% stake fee
10% unstake fee (collected in trx for instant TRX rewards and swapping to tokens for staking pool)
10 - 50% trx vault roll fee (10% for Stable & Crazy, 50% for Infinitew)
0% withdraw fee

What is TEWKEN token ?
TEWKEN is a TRC20 token running on the TRON blockchain
TEWKEN is scarce because its initial supply starts at 0 and caps at 100 million
Mining difficulty starts at 1 TRX and increases by 1 TRX every 2.5 million TEWKEN mined
Token address : https://tronscan.org/#/contract/TBhxyECmAg3uCqqmEHQvGJbrgj9cn1yMZ1

How to stake?

1) You have to approve 2 transactions (Approve and Stake)
2) First key in the amount of TEWKEN you want to stake. Then click Approve
3) Once the transaction has been mined, the Stake button will be enabled
4) Key in the amount you just approved. Then click Stake
