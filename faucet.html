FAQ

How to use this platform?
To use this platform on PC, TronLink or TronPay extension must be installed on your Google Chrome browser. If you are using smartphone TronWallet [iOS] or TronWallet [Android] is suitable. Put some TRX in your wallet and begin!
Tip:
When interacting with smart contracts, it's always better to freeze some TRX to gain Energy. This way energy will be consumed when triggering smart contract's functions, instead of TRX.
How does the game work?
Faucet allows players to double their deposits (200% returns).
50% of your initial deposit are used to purchased CRAZY tokens.
Remaining 50% of your deposit is paid out to existing players.
Dividends are generated while the contract holds on to CRAZY tokens.
Those dividends are distributed to all player, in the order they invested.
There is a deposit limit to prevent players from occupying the queue.
What are the fees?
There is a 2% developer fees
You will be refunded 80% of your initial deposit if the game were to cease operation
