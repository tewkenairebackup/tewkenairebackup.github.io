FAQ

How to use this platform?
To use this platform on PC, TronLink extension must be installed on your Google Chrome browser. If you are using smartphone TokenPocket [iOS] or TokenPocket [Android] is suitable. Put some TEWKEN in your wallet and begin!
Tip:
When interacting with smart contracts, it's always better to freeze some TRX to gain Energy. This way energy will be consumed when triggering smart contract's functions, instead of TRX.
How does the game work?
TEWKENITY is price inflationary token relative to TEWKEN and drips 2% of the reward pool daily
How does the card game work?
Players compete against one another in their respective team
The winning team shares the card reward pool
There are 32 cards available and starts at 10 TEWKEN each
When a card is purchased, the card doubles in price and adds 15 minutes to the main timer
Card price decreases (cut in half) after a 12 hour window if no one purchases that respective card
Card holders are rewarded a percentage of the reward pool at the end of the round
What are the fees?
50% entry fee (35% to reward pool + 5% instant rewards + 5% price appreciation + 4% card reward pool + 1% dev)
10% exit fee (5% to reward pool + 2% price appreciation + 2% card reward pool + 1% dev)
1% transfer fee
Card fee (85% previous card owner + 5% main reward pool + 5% ambassador + 4% to card reward pool + 1% dev)
What is the contract address?
https://tronscan.org/#/contract/TBDGCdVN2erATwrga4CPg2pYS9ALfT16vN
What is TEWKEN token ?
TEWKEN is a TRC20 token running on the TRON blockchain
TEWKEN is scarce because its initial supply starts at 0 and caps at 100 million
Mining difficulty starts at 1 TRX and increases by 1 TRX every 2.5 million TEWKEN mined
Token address : https://tronscan.org/#/contract/TBhxyECmAg3uCqqmEHQvGJbrgj9cn1yMZ1
