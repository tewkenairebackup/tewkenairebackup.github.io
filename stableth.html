FAQ

How to use this platform?
To use this platform on PC, Metamask extension must be installed on your Google Chrome browser. If you are using smartphone Metamask[Android] or Metamask[IOS] is suitable. Put some ETH in your wallet and begin!
Tip:
When interacting with smart contracts, it's always better to have some ETH for gas. The gas will be consumed when triggering smart contract's functions
How does the game work?
STABLE is pegged 1:1 to ETH (1 STABLE will always equal to 1 ETH). Dump proof
When you enter or exit, 10% is deducted from your deposit and distributed among STABLE holders
What are the fees?
10% entry fee (8% distribution + 1% crazy floor + 1% dev)
10% exit fee (8% distribution + 1% crazy floor + 1% dev)
1% transfer fee
