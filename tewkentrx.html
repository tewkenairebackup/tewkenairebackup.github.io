FAQ

How to use this platform?
To use this platform on PC, TronLink extension must be installed on your Google Chrome browser. If you are using smartphone TokenPocket [iOS] or TokenPocket [Android] is suitable. Put some TRX in your wallet and begin!
Tip:
When interacting with smart contracts, it's always better to freeze some TRX to gain Energy. This way energy will be consumed when triggering smart contract's functions, instead of TRX.
How does the farming game work?
Supply liquidity at  JustSwap  to obtain TEWKEN/TRX liquidity pool tokens
The farming contract drips 2% of the farming pool daily to TEWKEN/TRX farmers
250K - 1 million TEWKEN is allocated to the farming pool every 2 months
How does the card game work?
There are 32 cards available and starts at 5 TEWKEN/TRX each
When a card is purchased, the card doubles in price
Card price decreases (cut in half) after a 24 hour window if no one purchases that respective card
The card holders shares the card reward pools in real time (2% drip)
What are the fees?
There are no fees for the farming contract (stake, unstake, and withdraw)
Rolling to Tewkenity (50%). Rolling to Tewken Staking (0%)
Card fee (70% previous card owner + 20% tewken/trx holders + 9% card tewken/trx pool + 1% dev)
What is the contract address?
https://tronscan.org/#/contract/TJK7q3QufZqrH7vKVJc7XBvFCq5j8awJK3
What is TEWKEN token ?
TEWKEN is a TRC20 token running on the TRON blockchain
TEWKEN is scarce because its initial supply starts at 0 and caps at 100 million
Farming difficulty starts at 1 TRX and increases by 1 TRX every 2.5 million TEWKEN farmed
Token address : https://tronscan.org/#/contract/TBhxyECmAg3uCqqmEHQvGJbrgj9cn1yMZ1

How to stake?

1) Supply liquidity at JustSwap to obtain TEWKEN/TRX liquidity pool tokens
2) You have to approve 2 transactions (Approve and Stake)
3) Click Approve
4) Once the transaction has been mined, the Stake button will be enabled
5) Key in the amount of TEWKEN/TRX you want to stake. Then click Stake

How to unstake?

1) Enter the amount of TEWKEN/TRX tokens you want to unstake
2) Click Unstake
3) The unstaked tokens will appear in the Reward section (under TEWKEN/TRX)
4) Click Withdraw to transfer the tokens to your wallet
