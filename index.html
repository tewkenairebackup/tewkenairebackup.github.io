What is TEWKENAIRE?
Tewkenaire is a social experiment running on the blockchain. It is a community driven ecosystem of smart-contracts that circulates cryptocurrency rewards to its players.
What is TEWKEN STAKING?
The staking contract drips 2% of the staking pool daily to TEWKEN stakers
250k to 1 million TEWKEN is allocated to the staking pool every 2 months
The staking contract also rewards TRX as a bonus to stakers
INFINITEW and Crazy will provide external source of revenue to the contract
What is TEWKEN FARM?
Supply liquidity at JustSwap to obtain TEWKEN/TRX liquidity pool tokens
The farming contract drips 2% of the farming pool daily to TEWKEN/TRX farmers
250K - 1 million TEWKEN is allocated to the farming pool every 2 months
What is TEWKENITY?
TEWKENITY is price inflationary token relative to TEWKEN and drips 2% of the reward pool per day
What is OCTA?
OCTA is a reimagination of HEX with a low token supply
You can obtain OCTA by allocating your HEX to:
- The one time minting phase which runs for 30 days or
- Staking HEX (available after the minting phase ends)
